Mon, 31 Aug
Suluban Beaches → IHG Baruna
07:30
Breakfast at Rama Phala

Check-out Rama Phala, luggage in car

Drive south toward Uluwatu / Pecatu (~1.5h via ByPass Ngurah Rai)

Suluban Beach — hidden cove, cave entrance, stairs down to white sand
Free entry. Cave entrance at base of cliff. Great for photos. Low tide = larger beach area. 5 min drive from Uluwatu Temple area.
11:30
Padang Padang Beach — Eat Pray Love beach, white sand, turquoise water
~IDR 15K entry. Stairs down through cave. Can get busy but worth it for a quick visit. Toilets & changing rooms available.
12:30
Lunch at Single Fin Bali — cliff-top terrace, ocean views
Seafood, burgers, famous sunset spot. Great views even midday. 5 min from Suluban Beach.
14:00
Drive to IHG Baruna Tuban (~45 min-1h via ByPass)
15:00
Check-in IHG Baruna — Tuban (beachfront, 2 pools)
15:30
Pool / Relax / Freshen up
17:30
Sunset stroll on Kelan Beach (right outside IHG)
19:00
Farewell Dinner at Bawang Merah Beachfront (4.5, Kelan Beach, 5 min walk)
TripAdvisor Top 10% worldwide. Transparent pricing, fresh local seafood, beachfront dining. Much better than touristy Jimbaran. Last dinner in Bali!
