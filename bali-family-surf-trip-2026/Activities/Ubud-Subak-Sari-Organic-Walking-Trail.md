# Ubud Walking Trail: Subak & Sari Organic Walk

**Source:** Xiaohongshu / Google Maps screenshot (Jun 2026)

---

## Route Overview

A scenic walking loop through Ubud's iconic rice fields, weaving through the Subak irrigation system, past Sari Organic Walk, and along the Kajeng Rice Field area. Great for a morning or late afternoon walk.

## What's on the Route

| Landmark | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Sweet Orange Walk Trail** | Apparent trailhead/entry point at bottom of loop |
| **Sari Organic Walk** | Well-known organic farm/restaurant walking path through rice fields |
| **Kajeng Rice Field** | Popular photo spot (purple camera icon) |
| **Bridge accessing ricefield path** | Northern access point |
| **Indus Restaurant** | Beautiful view of Mount Agung |
| **Ibah** | Restaurant/cafe on the southwest side of loop |

## Nearby

- Karsa Spa (top left)
- Kastara Resort and Spa
- Jl. Raya Sanggingan (main road on west side)
- Jl. Suweta / Jl. Sri Wedari (main road on east side)

## Terrain

Lush green rice terraces, dense vegetation corridor (likely a river valley) running through the middle. Flanked by roads with resorts, spas, and restaurants. Typical Ubud countryside mix of agriculture and tourism infrastructure.

## Tip

The loop connects the main track (Sweet Orange -> Sari Organic -> Kajeng) with the western side near Indus Restaurant and Ibah, making a full circuit. Start early to avoid heat and crowds.
