# Route: Bali Timbungan → Wide Sands Beach Retreat (Day 1)

**Date:** Tuesday, Aug 25, 2026

## Route Summary

| | |
|---|---|
| **From** | Bali Timbungan, Jl. Sunset Road No.88, Kuta |
| **To** | Wide Sands Beach Retreat, Jalan Bahari, Pantai Yeh Sumbul, Medewi |
| **Distance** | 79.9 km |
| **Drive Time** | 2h 44min |
| **Route** | Via Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk |

## Directions Overview

### Segment 1: Bali Timbungan to Denpasar (34 min, 14.1 km)
1. Head west on Jl. Sunset Road
2. Take Jl. Glogor Carik northbound
3. Continue onto Jl. Mahendradatta
4. Follow signs to Jl. Cokroaminoto / Jl. HOS Cokroaminoto / Jl. Raya Sempidi
5. Enter Denpasar city center

### Segment 2: Denpasar to Yeh Sumbul via Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk (1h 46min, 64.9 km)
1. Merge onto Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk (main highway)
2. Continue northwest through Tabanan Regency
3. Pass through:
   - Mengwi
   - Sempidi
   - Dalung
   - Kediri
   - Pupuan
   - Marga
4. Continue on Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk toward Negara
5. Pass Negara town (largest town in Jembrana)
6. Continue toward Mendoyo
7. Turn off toward Yeh Sumbul / Medewi area

### Segment 3: Yeh Sumbul to Wide Sands Beach Retreat (3 min, 850m)
1. Follow local roads toward Pantai Yeh Sumbul
2. Look for Wide Sands Beach Retreat signage
3. Arrive at destination (beachfront property)

## Key Landmarks Along Route

- **Denpasar area:** Jl. Cokroaminoto, Jl. Raya Sempidi
- **Tabanan Regency:** Rice fields, temples
- **Negara:** Capital of Jembrana Regency (last major town before Medewi)
- **Mendoyo:** District center near Medewi
- **Yeh Sumbul:** Coastal village, Wide Sands location

## Road Conditions

- **Jl. Sunset Road:** Urban, moderate traffic
- **Jl. Mahendradatta:** Main arterial road, can be busy
- **Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk:** Major highway, 2-lane, good condition
- **Local roads to Medewi:** Narrower, rural, watch for motorcycles & pedestrians

## Timing Notes

**From Itinerary:**
- **Lunch at Bali Timbungan:** 1:30pm - 2:30pm
- **Depart for Medewi:** 2:30pm
- **Estimated arrival:** ~5:30pm (includes buffer for traffic, rest stops)

**Traffic Considerations:**
- Afternoon departure (2:30pm) avoids peak morning rush
- Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk is generally clear mid-afternoon
- Allow extra time for:
  - Denpasar city traffic
  - Motorcycles on rural roads
  - Possible road works or ceremonies

## Destination

**Wide Sands Beach Retreat**
- Address: Jalan Bahari, Pantai Yeh Sumbul Desa Yeh Sumbul, Medewi, Kec. Mendoyo, Kabupaten Jembrana, Bali 82261
- Type: Beachfront resort with 2 pools
- Check-in: 3pm (you'll arrive ~5:30pm)
- Features: Beach access, shallow water (great for kids), sunset views

## Next Steps (Day 1 Evening)

After check-in:
- **Afternoon:** Beach time / Pool
- **Sunset:** Medewi Point (3 min drive / 15 min walk)
- **Dinner:** Warung Gede (local seafood, ikan bakar, cash only)
