# Route: Ubud → Uluwatu (Day 6)

**Date:** Sunday, Aug 30, 2026

## Route Summary

| | |
|---|---|
| **From** | Pertiwi Resort & Spa, Ubud |
| **To** | ULU Garden, Uluwatu |
| **Distance** | 57.6 km |
| **Drive Time** | 2h 23min |
| **Route** | Via Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai (toll road) |

## Directions Overview

1. Head north on Jl. Monkey Forest (750m)
2. Turn right onto Jl. Raya Ubud (750m)
3. Continue via Jl. Sukma Kesuma → Jl. Peliatan I → Gg. Pekandelan
4. Turn left onto Jl. Raya Teges (8m)
5. Continue onto Jl. Cok Rai Pudak / Jl. Tjokorda Rai (4.5 km)
6. Via Jl. Raya Sakah → Jl. Raya Batuan → Jl. Raya Sukawati
7. Turn left onto Jl. Yudistira
8. Continue to Jl. Raya Guwang → Jl. Raya Ketewel
9. Turn right onto Jl. Prof. Dr. Ida Bagus Mantra (signs: Sanur/Denpasar)
10. Merge onto Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai (11.3 km)
11. **Toll Road:** Jl. Nusa Dua – Bandara Ngurah Rai – Benoa Toll Road
12. Take exit toward Bandara Ngurah Rai / Kuta
13. Turn left onto Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai (4.1 km)
14. Sharp right onto Jimbaran - Uluwatu / Jl. Uluwatu II (1.6 km)
15. Turn left onto Jl. Raya Uluwatu (7.7 km)
16. Turn right onto Jl. Labuansait (5.5 km)
17. **Arrive:** ULU Garden (destination on right)
