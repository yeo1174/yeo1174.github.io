## Segment 1: Wide Sands Beach Retreat → Jatiluwih Rice Terraces

| | |
|---|---|
| **Distance** | 68.4 km |
| **Drive Time** | 2h 5min |

### Directions

1. **Continue to Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk / Jl. Raya Jembrana** (2 min, 850m)
   - Exit Wide Sands Beach Retreat
   - Turn onto local road toward main highway
   - Merge onto Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk

2. **Continue on Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk to Meliling** (1h 18min, 44.0 km)
   - Head east on main highway
   - Pass through:
     - Mendoyo
     - Negara (Jembrana capital)
     - Marga
     - Pupuan
   - Continue toward Kediri/Meliling area

3. **Continue on Jl. Raya Timpag to Jatiluwih** (45 min, 23.5 km)
   - Turn north onto Jl. Raya Timpag (signs to Jatiluwih)
   - Mountain road ascent (scenic rice terraces appear)
   - Follow signs to Jatiluwih Rice Terraces entrance
   - Arrive at parking area

### Jatiluwih Rice Terraces (Stopover)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Address** | Penebel, Tabanan Regency, Bali, Indonesia |
| **UNESCO Status** | World Heritage Site (2012) |
| **Entrance Fee** | ~IDR 40,000 per person (adults) |
| **Opening Hours** | 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM |
| **Parking** | Available at entrance |
| **Facilities** | Toilets, small warungs, viewpoint platforms |

### Recommended Stop Duration

| Activity | Time |
|----------|------|
| Parking & entry | 10 min |
| Walk to viewpoint | 10 min |
| Photos & exploration | 30-45 min |
| Return to car | 10 min |
| **Total** | **~1 hour** |

### Why Stop Here?

- ✅ **UNESCO World Heritage Site** - Subak irrigation system
- ✅ **Stunning panoramic views** of terraced rice paddies
- ✅ **Family photo opportunity** - iconic Bali landscape
- ✅ **Kids can explore** - safe walking paths, open spaces
- ✅ **Cooler mountain climate** - refreshing break from coast
- ✅ **Traditional Balinese culture** - working rice farms
- ✅ **Less crowded** than Tegalalang (Ubud area)

### Suggested Timing

- **Depart Wide Sands:** 12:00 PM (after check-out)
- **Arrive Jatiluwih:** 2:00 PM
- **Lunch:** Pack snacks OR eat at small warungs near entrance
- **Visit:** 2:15 PM - 3:15 PM (1 hour)
- **Depart Jatiluwih:** 3:15 PM
- **Arrive Ubud:** 4:30 - 5:00 PM

## Segment 2: Jatiluwih Rice Terraces → Pertiwi Resort & Spa

| | |
|---|---|
| **Distance** | 39.2 km |
| **Drive Time** | 1h 24min |

### Directions

1. **Take Jl. Cokro Aminoto and Jl. Jatiluwih-Mengesta to Jl. Raya Babahan Senganan in Penebel** (26 min, 10.7 km)
   - Exit Jatiluwih parking area
   - Head south on Jl. Cokro Aminoto
   - Continue onto Jl. Jatiluwih-Mengesta
   - Arrive at Jl. Raya Babahan Senganan

2. **Take Jl. Raya Buruan and Jl. Buruan to Jl. Wisnu Marga in Tunjuk** (11 min, 6.4 km)
   - Turn onto Jl. Raya Buruan
   - Continue onto Jl. Buruan
   - Merge onto Jl. Wisnu Marga

3. **Continue on Jl. Wisnu Marga. Take Jl. Abimanyu to Jl. Raya Sangeh in Sangeh** (15 min, 7.0 km)
   - Continue on Jl. Wisnu Marga
   - Take Jl. Abimanyu
   - Arrive at Jl. Raya Sangeh

4. **Turn right onto Jl. Raya Sangeh** (8 min, 5.0 km)
   - Head east on Jl. Raya Sangeh
   - Continue through Sangeh area (monkey forest nearby)

5. **Continue on Jl. Legong. Take Jl. Baris to Jl. Raya Semana** (5 min, 2.8 km)
   - Continue onto Jl. Legong
   - Take Jl. Baris
   - Arrive at Jl. Raya Semana

6. **Continue on Jl. Raya Semana. Take Jl. Raya Singakerta, Jl. Raya Nyuh Kuning and Jl. Raya Pengosekan Ubud to Jl. Monkey Forest in Ubud** (19 min, 7.3 km)
   - Continue on Jl. Raya Semana
   - Take Jl. Raya Singakerta
   - Continue onto Jl. Raya Nyuh Kuning
   - Merge onto Jl. Raya Pengosekan Ubud
   - Turn onto Jl. Monkey Forest
   - **Arrive Pertiwi Resort & Spa** (destination on right)

## Key Landmarks Along Route

### Segment 1 (Medewi → Jatiluwih)
- **Medewi:** Starting point, surfing beach
- **Negara:** Capital of Jembrana Regency
- **Pupuan:** Junction area, mountain views begin
- **Meliling:** Gateway to Jatiluwih highlands
- **Jatiluwih:** UNESCO rice terraces, mountain elevation (~700m)

### Segment 2 (Jatiluwih → Ubud)
- **Penebel:** District center near Jatiluwih
- **Tunjuk:** Village en route to Ubud
- **Sangeh:** Known for monkey forest (alternative to Ubud Monkey Forest)
- **Ubud outskirts:** Jl. Raya Semana, Jl. Singakerta
- **Monkey Forest Road:** Final approach to Pertiwi Resort

## Road Conditions

| Road Section | Condition | Notes |
|--------------|-----------|-------|
| **Jl. Raya Denpasar - Gilimanuk** | Good, 2-lane highway | Main arterial, moderate traffic |
| **Jl. Raya Timpag (to Jatiluwih)** | Good, narrower | Mountain road, winding, scenic |
| **Jatiluwih area roads** | Good, rural | Some steep sections |
| **Jl. Wisnu Marga → Ubud** | Good, 2-lane | Moderate traffic near Ubud |
| **Jl. Monkey Forest** | Urban, busy | Watch for motorcycles, pedestrians |

### Driving Tips

- ⚠️ **Mountain roads:** Slower speeds, watch for oncoming traffic
- ⚠️ **Motorcycles:** Very common, give space
- ⚠️ **Ubud traffic:** Can be congested, especially late afternoon
- ⚠️ **Parking at Jatiluwih:** Arrive before 3 PM for easier parking
- ✅ **Fuel:** Fill up in Negara or before leaving Medewi
- ✅ **Rest stops:** Jatiluwih is perfect mid-journey break
